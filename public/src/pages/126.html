Nkwetaaga Bulijjo
I Need Thee Every Hour
An
3

R Lowry
Doh is Ab 
1. Nkwetaaga bulijjo, Ayi- Ye-su;
Sirina mirembe, Wantu wo-nna.

Nkwetaaga ayi Yesu, Bulijjo nkwetaaga;
Ompe omukisa gwo, Jjangu- gyendi.

2. Netaaga obeere, Kumpi- na-nge;
Ebikemo byonna, Ggw(e) obi-go-ba.

3. Nkwetaaga mu ssanyu, Ne mu-nna-ku,
Ggwe obeere nange, Nnem(e) o-ku-fa.

4. Onjigirizenga, By'oya-ga-la,
Ebisuubizo byo, Bibe- mu- nze.

5. Ekiseera kyonna, Ayi Mu-ka-ma,
Netaaga onfuule, Omwana-na-wo.
