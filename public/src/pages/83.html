Tuba n'Essanyu mu Mwoyo
Sweet Sabbath School
An
4
4
W H Doane
Doh is Ab 
1. Tuba n'essanyu mu- mwo-yo, Erisinga lyo-nna,
Ku lunaku lwa Ssa-bbii-ti Nga tusinza Yesu.

Ssabbiiti ya Yesu, Lunaku lwa ssanyu!
Ku lunaku lwa Ssa-bbii-ti Tusinza Mukama.

2. Endwadde zomwoyo- zo-nna, Kwe ziwonyeze-bwa,
Omwo mwe nnonya e-ssanyu N'eddembe ly'omwoyo.

3. Mbeera ne Mukama- wa-nge, Ng'ampita n'eki-sa,
Ye n'ampa okumwa-ga-la, N'omutima gwange.
