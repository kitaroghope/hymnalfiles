Ka Tumusinze Mukama Waffe
O Worship the King
An
3
4
J.M.Haydn
Doh is G 
1. Katumusinze Mukama waffe, 
Katuyimbe ku kwaga-l(a) okungi.
Oyo yekka kye kiddukiro kyaffe, 
Ekitiibwa kyonna kimuwe-toloola.

2. Tuyimbe ku kisa kye ekingi, 
(E)byambalo bye gwe (o)musa-na gwensi;
Okubwatuka kwe kutiisa byonna, 
Amakubo ge gali ne- mu mpewo.

3. Obuyinza bwe era nentiisa, 
Birabikira wonna- ku bire;
Eggulu neraddu nomuyag(a) era, 
Byonna baddu be abamwu-wereza.

4. Wansi weggulu (O)mugulumivu, 
Wateekebwa dda enku-luze yo;
Ye nsi gye watonda nogyetolooza, 
Ennyanja zonna nga ensa-lo zaayo.

5. Ani ayinza okunnyumiza, Ebyo, 
(O)mugabi waffe,- by'ogaba?
Eby'ekisa kyo, byebitwewunyisa: 
Katonda, Mulokoz(i), era- Kabaka!
