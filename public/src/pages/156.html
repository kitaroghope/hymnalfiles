SDAH 538
Katonda Omutukuvu
Guide Me. O Thou Great Jehovah
4
4
John Hughes
Doh is G 
1. Katonda Omu-tu-kuvu, Nnu'ngamya nga ntambula!
Ndi munafu, Ggwe- o-lina amaanyi ge- netaaga;
Mpanirira, Nywereza nze, Mu mukon(o) ogw'amaanyi,
Mu muko-n(o) o-gw'amaanyi.

2. Gulaw(o) ensulo-(e)y'a-mazzi, Etukuz(e) abaana bo,
Giggulire entukuze, Okummalam(u) ekibi.
Gwe empagi y'omuliro, Beera engabo yange,
Beera e-nga-bo yange.

3. Bwe ndituuka ku- mba-lama Z'omugga Yoludaani,
Okutya kwange- ku-ggwewo, Ontuse mu- Kanaani!
Neyonger(e) ennaku zonna, (O)kukutenderezanga,
(O)kukute-nde-rezanga.
