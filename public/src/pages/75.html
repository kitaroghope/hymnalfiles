1. Omusingi gwaffe munywe-vu ddala,-
Kye kigambo kya- Yes(u) ekitukuvu;

[Fuula-nga Yesu ekidddu-kiro kyo.-]x2

2. Bw'onoyitanga mu nnyanja- totyanga,-
Mu migg(a) egy'enna-ku tewekanganga:
Kubanga nnaabeeranga wa-mu naawe,--
[Nnaaku-wonyanga mu nnaku zo- zonna.-]x2

3. "Mu bikemo ebiri mu- kkubo lyo,-

[Naye olivaamu ng'olongo-sebbwa.-]x2
