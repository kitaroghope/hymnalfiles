Nsanyukira Nnyo Okuyimba
I Have a Song I Love to Sing
4
4
E.O.EXCEL
Doh is G 
1. Nsanyukiranny(o) o-kuyimba Eby'obulokozi;
Ku Kabaka- wa-nge Yesu, Eyannunula nze

E-yannunula nze, E-yannunula nze
Oyo lye ssanyu lyange; E-yannunula nze,
Nsanyuka nnyo olw'erinnya lye.

2. Nnina Yesu- a-mpa byonna, Eyannunula nze;
Nsanyuk(a) o-ku-muwereza Eyannunula nze.

3. Ye Mujulirw(a) o-mwesigwa Eyannunula nze;
Leero sikya-li-ko kye ntya Yannunula Oyo.

4. Antegeke-dde amaka Eyannunula nze;
Mwe nnaabeera-nga- bulijjo Eyannunula nze.
