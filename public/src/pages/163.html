1. Oba ekiseera kirituuka ku kya
Okumasamasa kwe ne kulabika;-
Yesu ng'ajja n'ekitiibwa ekinene
Okututwala ewuwe.

Ayi Mukama, Olija ddi?
Tulab(e) amaaso go!
Yesu ajja, Aleruya
Aleruya! Amiina
Aleruya! Amiina.

2. Buliba mu ttuntu obla) eyo mu ttumbi, --
Tuliba tul(i) awo ngensi yonna, yonna; --
Efuuka omusana ogwaka ennyo,
Mukama ng'atuddu-kidde.

3. Bamalayika nga bayimba nesssanyu, ---
Era nabanunule be bonna wamu ---
Nekitibwa kye ekyakayakan(a) ennyo,

4. Tuligenda n'essanyu eteri kufa, - --
Teri ndwadde, Teri nnaku, terintiisa;
Tuliyita mu bire wamu ne Yesu,
Ng'atutwala ffe e-wuwe
