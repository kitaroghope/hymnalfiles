Ffe Twayitibwa Ku Mbaga
Called to the Feast
4
4
E S Lorenz
Doh is Eb 
1. Ffe twayitibwa ku mbaga ye, Tutuule wamu n'abantu be,
Kale nno tulibeera tutya, Kabaka ng'azze?

Kale owo-luganda, Yesu- lw'alijja,
Olibeera ku ludda ki, Kabaka ng'azze?

2. Ng'atikidd(e) engule ku mutwe, Nekitiibw(a) oyo eyattibwa,
Entiisa eribaawo nnene, Kabaka ng'azze.

3. Alijja wamu nokumyansa, Byonna bye tukisa abantu;
Birimanyibwa buli muntu, Kabaka ng'azze.

4. Alisanyukira ababe, Abaliba mu ngoy(e) enjeru;
Tugume tuwangule byonna, Kabaka ng'azze.

5. Mukama otuwe ekisa, Fenna tukulindirirenga;
Tusanyuke okukulaba, Bwonooba ng'ozze.
