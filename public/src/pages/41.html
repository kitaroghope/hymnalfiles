Omwoyo Omutukuvu
Holy Spirit, Faithful Guide
3

W.A.F.Schulthes
Doh is G 
1. Omwoyo Omutu-kuvu, Omulungamya- waffe
Tukwate ku mu-kono Fenna abata-mbuze,
Abalina e-nnaku Tuwulirize bulungi,
Eddobooz(i) eddu-ngi nti: "Mujje, nze maba-tuusa".

2. Omubeezi (o)mwe- sigwa, Otuyambe bu-lijjo,
Totuleka mu- ntiisa Ey'enzikiza y'ensi.
(O)tusanyuse mu- nnaku Bwe tunawuliranga,
Eddobooz(i) eddu-ngi nti: "Mujje, nze naba-tuusa."

3. (O)lugendo bwe lu-liggwa, Nga tulindiri-ra Ggwe,
Eby'ensi bwe bi-riggwa, Ng'olin(a) amannya gaffe,
(O)tuyise mu ma-yengo, (O)lw'omusayi gwa Yesu,
Eddobooz(i) eddu-ngi nti: "Mujje, nze naba-tuusa".
