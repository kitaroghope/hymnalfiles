Nze Omwonoonyi Lukulwe
Chief of Sinners
4
4
Arr by Benjamin Carr
Doh is Ab 
1. Nz(e) omwonoonyi lukulwe, Yesu yafa ku lwange;
Yafa mbeere mu ggulu, Yafa nze nneme kufa;
Ng'ettabi ku zzabbibu, Ndi wuwe naye wange.

2. (O)Kwagala kwe kungi nnyo, Kutuuka waggulu nnyo;
Era kukka wansi nnyo, Kwo kwa mirembe gyonna;
Yannoonya n'anzuula nze, Nga sinnamukowoola!

3. Omwonoonyi lukulwe, Kri-sto ye byonna gyendi;
Amanyi byeneetaaga, Annumirwak(o) ennaku;
Nze bwemba naye sitya, Abeera ngabo yange.
