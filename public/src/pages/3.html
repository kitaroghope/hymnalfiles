Aweebwe Ekitiibwa
To God be the Glory
An
3
4
W.H.Doane
Doh is Ab 
1. Awebw(e) ekitiibwa Katonda waffe; 
Yayagal(a) ensi n'awaay(o) Omwana we,
Yafuuka saddaaka y'ekibi kyaffe; 
Naggulaw(o) oluggi ffe tuyingire.

Tumutendereze, Ensi ewulire; 
Tuyimbe tuyimbe, Ensi esanyuke;
Mujj(e) eri Kitaffe mu Mwana Yesu; 
Aweebw(e) ekitiibwa yatununula.

2. Twanunulibwa twagulwa musaayi; 
Kisuubizo eri buli akkiriza;
Omubi lukulwe amukkiriza, 
Asonyiyibwa ebibi bye byonna.

3. Atuyigirizz(a) ebikulu bingi, 
N'essanyu lingi lyatuwa mu Yesu;
Oyo Mutukuvu mugulumivu, 
Kiriba kisuffu nga tumulabye.
