Yesu Yasuubiza Abaana Be
Sweet Promise Is Given
An
3
4
E E Belden
Doh is G 
1. Yesu vasuubiza abakkiriza,
Nti "Ndikomawo ne nembatwala gyendi;
Mutunule nga munnindirira,
(O)kutuusa lwe ndijja, mwe munywerenga."

"Mwe munywerenga," Bweyasuubiza;
"Ndibawa eggulu n'ensi empya;
Era mulifuga wamu nange, Muliweebw(a) engule,
Mwe munywerenga."

2. Katutunule nga tusabanga,
Alijja mu ssaawa etamanviddwa;
Tumanyi nga ali kumpi ddala;
Olwobubonero bweyatuw(a) edda.

3. Essuubi lyaffe mu kigambo kye -
Kwekukomawo kwa Yesu kans(i) eno
Kyekisinga byonna obukulu,
Ffe ka tunywerenga, Alijja mangu.
