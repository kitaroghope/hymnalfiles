Nsanyukira Ekigambo Kino
I Am So Glad That Our Father
6
8
P P Bliss
Doh is G 
1. Nsanyukira ekigambo kino,
Yesu Mukama atwagala nnyo;
Birimu bingi ebinsanyusa,
Eky(o) ekisinga nti "Nze nkwagala."

Anjagala, Ye anjagala, Kya kital(o) Okunjagala!
Anjagala olw'ekisa kye, Nang(e) era mwagala.

2. Bwemba nnyonoona ne mwerabira,
Yesu tandeka kubula ddala;
Ampita mangu (o)kumweyuna Ye,
Er(a) anjjijukiz(a) olwagala kwe.

3. Bwe nditunulir(a) ekitiibwa kye,
Ndiyimba ntya bwe siyimba bwentyo?
Nnyimba emirembe n’emirembe,
Yesu kik(i) ekyakunjagaza nze?

4. Anjagala era mmwagala Ye;
Yakka mu ns(i) olw’okutufiirira;
(O)kwagala kwamuleeta ku muti
Yesu atwagala ffe bwagazi.
