Annung'amya Kya Ssanyu
He Leadeth
An
4
4
Wm B Bradbury
Doh is D 
1. Annung'amya, kinsanyusa! Kigambo kimpa- amaanyi!
Buli kyenkola, gyembeera, (O)mu-kono gwe-gu-nu-ng'amya.

Annung'amya, annung'-amya, (O)mukono gwe-gunnung'amya;
Ka mmwesige ngobere-re, Kubanga Ye-a-nnu-ng'amya.

2. Bwe mbeera mu nnaku ennyingi, Oba bwembeera- mu ssanyu;
Mu ddembe oba mu kabi, (O)mu-kono gwe- gu-nu-ng'amya.

3. Mukama nnywez(a) omukono, Sigenda kwemmu-lugunya;
Mbeera mumativu wonna, Ku-banga Ggwe- o-nu-ng'amya.

4. Bwendimala omulimu, Mu kisa kyo nga- mpangudde;
Okufa nze sirikutya, Ku-banga Ggwe- o-nu-ng'amya.
