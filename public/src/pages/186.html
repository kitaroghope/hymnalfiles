Mujje Nga Mweterereka
(tune-Our Blessed Redeemer) Rest in the Lord
4
4
J B Dykes
Doh is Eb 
1. Mujje, nga mweterereka
Mu maaso ga Yesu;
Okwagala kwe kujjuze
(E)myoyo gyammwe

2. Wulira ggw(e) alindirira
(O)mugolewo leero,
Mu kwagal(a) okutaggwawo
Mugattibwe.

3. Naawe akkirizza wano
Okumufumbirwa,
Laba Kitaffe awulira
(O)bweyamo bwo!

4. Nammwe ababetoolodde,
Okubasabira,
Katonda waff(e) akakasa
(E)birayiro.

5. Mujje nga mweterereka:
Mukama waffe ajja,
Okutusembeza gyali,
Mugolewe.

6. Bwe mutyo mweterereke:
Agabire bonna
Omwoyo Omutukuvu
Abasaba.
