CH 588
Kirungi Okwesiga Yesu
'Tis so Sweet to Trust in Jesus'
4
4
Won J Kirkpatrick
Doh is Ab 
1. Kirung(i) okwesiga Yesu, N'ekigambo kye kyonna;
N'ebyo Ye bye yasuubiza, Ebyava mu kamwa ke.

Yesu, Yesu, nga mmwesiga; Nga mmukakasizza nnyo!
Yesu, Yesu, Omulungi! Mp(a) ekisa nkwesigenga!

2. Kirung(i) okwesiga Yesu, Twesig(e) omusaayi gwe;
Tukkirize tunaazibwe, Mu nsulo etukuza.

3. Kirung(i) okwesiga Yesu, So si bikolwa byaffe,
Mu Ye tufun(e) obulamu, Essanyu n'emirembe.

4. Nsanyuka, Kuba mmwesiga, Yesu Ow'omukwano;
Era mmanyi ali nange, (O)kutuus(a) enkomerero.
