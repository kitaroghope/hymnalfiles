CH 446
Yesu Mukama Wange
Work for the Night is Coming
4
4
Lowell Mason
Doh is F 
1. Yesu Mukama wange, Nesig(a) amaanyi go;
Sitya balabe bange, Ne bwe bannumba;
Onkuum(a) ennyo lwa ki-sa, Siriko kye ntidde
Mu ngalo za Kitange Mwe mpummulira.

2. Setaani bw'ab(a) annumba Nettima lye lingi;
Buli lw'ajj(a) okunnimba, Nziramu bwe nti,
"Vaawo genda- Setaani, Yesu yakusinga;
Nali nkusenze edda, Ne nkusenguka'.

3. Omulokozi Yesu, Tewali ayinza,
Okutusikula ffe Mu mikono gyo;
Alituloopa y(e) a ni? Ggwe watununula,
Wawangula Setani, Tetukyamutya.
